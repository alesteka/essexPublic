Course outline 1

In the following module we will examine the ethical responsibilities of Computer Science Professionals and will be able to understand relevant computer applications, hardware and software perspectives.

Through understanding of relevant theories and programming concepts we will be able to undertake real world scenarios. Overall purpose is to develop an ethos of proffesional development and be able to understand computer's architecture, emerging threats, social implications and recent inovations in Computer Science.
